Roasted Cauliflower Pasta

Description

Cheese- and garlic-packed pasta, roasted cauliflower, and a crisp panko crust will take you straight to carb heaven. Maybe it’s because they’re both white, or perhaps it’s because they both tend to take in flavors from their surroundings, or maybe it’s because they both have a strong affinity for cheese. Whatever the reason, pasta and cauliflower just go together, yet people don’t put them together often enough. And by “people” I mean people other than me. I do it a lot.
